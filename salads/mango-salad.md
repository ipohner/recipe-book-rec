# Mango salad
 
## Ingredients
 
- 3 ripe **mangoes**
- 200 g **cherry tomatoes**
- 2-3 **spring onions**
- 1 fresh **red chili pepper** (or optionally pepper flakes)
- Lime juice, freshly pressed
- Sprinkle of **olive oil**
 
## Instructions
 
- Peel the mangoes and slice them into bite-sized chunks.
- Add the lime juice.
- Cut the cherry tomates in halves or slices and add to the bowl.
- Slice the spring onions into thin rings and add them.
- Remove the seeds from the pepper (or keep them, if you like the extra heat) and dice it finely, then add everything to the salad.
- Finish it off with a bit of olive oil and mix everything well.
- The salad tastes best after some time in the refrigerator. Chill for at least 1-2 hours, ideally overnight before serving.
